Who We Are

Redefining Packaging
for a Greener World

BeyondPlastic is a pioneering compostable packaging company headquartered in Hyderabad, Telangana. We are on a mission to replace conventional single-use plastics with certified compostable alternatives that are kind to the earth.

Founded by passionate environmentalists and industry veterans, we manufacture a wide range of bags — from retail carry bags to hospital-grade aprons — all made from certified compostable raw materials sourced from the finest granule suppliers.

We are proud holders of BIS certification and comply with IS/ISO 17088 standards for compostable plastics. Every product we manufacture is tested for compostability, tensile strength, and safety — ensuring that our customers receive only the best.

Our Process

From Granules to Greener Bags

A transparent look at how we manufacture our compostable bags — step by step.

01

Sourcing Certified Granules

We begin by sourcing certified compostable granules — primarily PBAT (Polybutylene Adipate Terephthalate) and PLA (Polylactic Acid) — from trusted, certified suppliers. These granules are fully compostable and comply with international composting standards including EN 13432 and ASTM D6400.

  • PBAT + PLA Blend Granules
  • EN 13432 Certified Materials
  • Rigorous Supplier Audits
02

Mixing & Compounding

The granules are precisely blended with natural additives — including starch, calcium carbonate, and organic pigments — to achieve the optimal strength, flexibility, and printability. Our in-house compounding machines ensure a homogeneous mix that delivers consistent quality in every batch.

  • Precision Ratio Blending
  • Natural Color Additives
  • Batch Consistency Checks
03

Blown Film Extrusion

The compounded material is fed into our state-of-the-art blown film extrusion machines. The mixture is melted and extruded through a circular die to form thin, uniform film tubes. Air is blown into the tube to expand it to the required width and thickness — creating rolls of compostable film.

  • Precision Thickness Control
  • Multiple Gauge Options
  • Continuous Quality Monitoring
04

Bag Cutting & Sealing

Printed film rolls are fed into our cutting and sealing machines that precisely cut and heat-seal the bags to the required dimensions. Whether it's a U-cut, D-cut, W-cut, or gusset bag — our machines are programmed for exact tolerances ensuring every bag meets spec with perfect seals.

  • Heat Seal Technology
  • Multiple Bag Styles
  • Automated Counting & Stacking
05

Printing & Customisation

The film rolls are then processed through our flexographic printing machines. We use soy-based and water-based inks that are environmentally safe and vibrant. Customers can have their branding, logos, and instructions printed directly on the bags with up to 8 colours.

  • Eco-Safe Soy-Based Inks
  • Up to 8-Colour Printing
  • Custom Sizing & Branding
06

Quality Control & Dispatch

Every batch undergoes rigorous quality checks — including tensile strength testing, seal integrity verification, compostability verification, and dimensional accuracy. Only bags that pass all parameters are packed and dispatched to our clients. We ensure on-time delivery across India.

  • Tensile & Seal Testing
  • Compostability Verified
  • Pan-India Dispatch
