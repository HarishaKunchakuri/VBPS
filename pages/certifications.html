Our Certifications

At Beyond Plastic, every product is manufactured in full compliance with national and international biodegradability standards. Our certifications reflect our unwavering commitment to quality, environmental responsibility, and consumer safety.

BIS Certification (IS 17899)

Bureau of Indian Standards certification confirming that our compostable carry bags comply with IS 17899 — the Indian standard for compostable plastic carry bags. This is mandatory for all biodegradable bag manufacturers in India.

View Certificate

ISO 17088 – Compostable Plastics

International standard certification (ISO/IS 17088) for specifications relating to compostable plastics. Our products have passed laboratory biodegradability, disintegration, and eco-toxicity tests as required under this standard.

View Certificate

CPCB Registration

Registered with the Central Pollution Control Board (CPCB), India, under the Plastic Waste Management Rules, 2016. Our registration confirms that we are a legally compliant manufacturer of compostable plastic products.

View Certificate
