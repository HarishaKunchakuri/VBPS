Milestones

Our Achievements

Key milestones in our journey to make packaging greener.

🏆

Best Green Enterprise 2023

Awarded by CII (Confederation of Indian Industry) for outstanding contribution to sustainable manufacturing in Telangana.

📜

BIS Certification Achieved

Successfully obtained Bureau of Indian Standards certification under IS 17899 for compostable carry bags — one of the first in Telangana.

🤝

Government Partnership

Empanelled as an approved supplier for Telangana State government's Swachh Bharat biodegradable bag distribution initiative.

🏥

Hospital Industry Tie-Up

Signed supply agreements with 3 major hospital chains in Hyderabad for biodegradable aprons and head caps — replacing conventional plastic.

🌱

1 Crore Bags Milestone

Celebrated the milestone of manufacturing and supplying over 1 crore compostable bags since inception — preventing tonnes of plastic waste.

📰

Featured in Deccan Chronicle

Our story of pioneering biodegradable packaging in Hyderabad was featured in a special edition article by Deccan Chronicle on World Environment Day.
